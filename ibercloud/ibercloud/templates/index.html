IberGrid & IberCloud

IberGrid is an collaborative agreement established between Portuguese NGI (PT-NGI) and Spanish NGI (ES-NGI) for setting a common umbrella for the distributed service deployment and effort sharing on national and global EGI tasks.

IberCloud is the IberGrid initiative to provide access to resources via cloud mode.

If you are interested in getting access request an account by filling a form with your computing needs

Use it!

The IberCloud sites use a common LDAP authentication server for providing single sign-on to the resources. Check the complete list of resources and access APIs at the Ibergrid wiki. We are working on extending our infrastructure to more sites
